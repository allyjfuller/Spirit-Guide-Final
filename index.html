About

Spirit Guide was developed by seasoned New York City bartender, Ally Fuller. Through her years of experience working in many different bars in cities around the world so noticed a key aspect of bartending that bridged even the most drastic culture gaps: Recipe Consistancy. A margarita should taste just as good in Tel Aviv as it does on the shores of Tulum, Mexico. From this mission, Spirit Guide was born.

Now exists a web-based app that bartenders around the world can access, creating global consistency in the quality of cocktails being served.


Features

Here's a taste of what we have to offer:

Cocktail Search

Search any cocktail by name and trade in your disorganized, inconvenient paper rolladex for a fast and easy way to look up recipes on your mobile phone or POS system.

Save Favorites

Dreaming up a new cocktail menu? Inspired by the classics? Or maybe you have a regular who simply LOVES to drink Zombies the same way every time. Save any cocktail to your favorites page for easy access.
